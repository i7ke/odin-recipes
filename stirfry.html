StirFry

Description

This healthy stir fry recipe is quick and easy and guaranteed to impress friends and family!

Dont focus too much on making it look good. That will come naturally! This will surely taste as good as it looks!

Ingredients

  1. 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast cut into 1 inch cubes
  2. salt and pepper to taste
  3. 2 tbsp olive oil divided
  4. 2 cups broccoli florets
  5. 1/2 yellow bell pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
  6. 1/2 red bell pepper cut into 1 inch pieces
  7. 1/2 cup baby carrots sliced
  8. 2 tsp minced ginger
  9. 2 garlic cloves minced

Stir Fry Sauce

  1. 1 tbsp corn starch
  2. 2 tbsp cold water
  3. 1/4 cup low sodium chicken broth
  4. 3 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  5. 1/4 cup honey
  6. 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  7. 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
